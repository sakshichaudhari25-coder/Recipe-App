🥗 healthy recipe card 🥗

A recipe is a guide that helps us prepare food step by step. It tells us what ingredients we need and how to mix them to make tasty dishes. Recipes make cooking easier, healthier, and more fun 😄.

🍓Fruit salad🍓

Ingredients🍄‍🟫
  • 1 medium apple (chopped)
  • 1 banana (sliced)
  • 1 orange (peeled and segmented) OR ½ cup orange segments
  • ½ cup grapes (halved)
  • 1 Tbsp honey (optional)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 2 Tbsp chopped nuts (almonds/walnuts) — optional

TIP 🥦
Prepare just before serving for best texture. Store in the fridge up to 1 day🫶.

Calories: 150 kcal per serving

🍲Vegetable soup🍲

Ingredients🥕
  • 1 Tbsp oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 carrot, diced
  • 1 small potato, diced (optional)
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • ½ cup peas/beans (fresh or frozen)
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 3–4 cups vegetable broth or water
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • ½ tsp dried herbs (oregano/thyme) — optional
  • Fresh coriander or parsley for garnish

TIP 🥦
Add cooked lentils or diced tofu for extra protein. Keeps 2–3 days in fridge 🫶

Calories: 180 kcal per serving

🥙Sprouts salad🥙

Ingredients
  • 1 cup moong (green gram) sprouts, washed
  • 1 small tomato, chopped
  • ½ cucumber, chopped
  • ¼ small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 green chili, finely chopped (optional)
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt to taste
  • 1–2 Tbsp chopped coriander
  • ¼ tsp chaat masala (optional)

TIP 🥦
Raw sprouts can sometimes carry bacteria. If serving to kids, pregnant women, elderly, or immune-compromised people, lightly cook the sprouts before using 🫶.

Calories: 180 kcal per serving

🍹Carrot-Orange Juice🍹

Ingredients🥕
  • 2 large carrots
  • 2 oranges
  • 1/2 lemon (optional for extra tang)
  • A pinch of black salt (optional)

Approximately 175 calories for a full glass of Carrot-Orange Juice (about 300–350ml).
